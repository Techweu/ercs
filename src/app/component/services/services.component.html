ISI / BIS Certification (ISI Mark) – Scheme I

ISI Certification is the standard compliance mark for industrial products issued by Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS). We provide hassle-free ISI Certification Services to the product manufacturers.

BIS Registration (CRS) – Scheme II

BIS Registration is mandatory for Electronic and IT products, LED Lights, Solar Photovoltaics, Switchgears, Chemicals, Cotton, etc. We assist you in getting quick BIS Registration Services for Indian Market.

BIS / ISI Certification for Foreign Manufacturers (FMCS)

Foreign Manufacturers Certification Scheme (FMCS) is a Scheme of BIS / ISI Certification of product for foreign manufacturers. We deliver smooth end-to-end BIS / ISI Certification Services (FMCS) for Foreign Manufacturers.

WPC Approval (ETA)

WPC Approval is a Safety Certification for Wireless products like headphone, speakers, smartwatch, smart TV, etc. being consumed in India. We bring you fast WPC approval (ETA) Services for better marketplace.

BEE Registration (Star Ratings)

BEE Registration (Star Ratings) is a power consumption rating of household electrical appliances like air conditioners, refrigerator, geysers, etc. Our BEE Registration Services help your product to get BEE certified.

EPR Registration for Plastic Waste

EPR Registration for Plastic Waste is mandatory for recycling plastic waste in India. Our distinctive EPR Registration Services will get you product EPR certified swiftly.
